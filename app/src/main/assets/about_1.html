The Superintendence of Securities Markets (SMV) is a public institution that seeks to ensure the protection of investors, the efficiency and transparency of the markets under their supervision, as well as the orderly pricing and dissemination of all information necessary for such purposes, by availing ourselves of robust regulation, supervision and promotion to meet our objectives.

A key challenge is to contribute to the development of Peru's securities market given the role it plays in channeling resources to the economy. To that end, we facilitate the access of economic agents to an array of financial instruments and financial investments, while informing them of the benefits and the risks of both.
