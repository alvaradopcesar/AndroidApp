The SMV is delighted to be hosting the 41st Annual Conference of the International Organization of Securities Commissions (IOSCO), taking place from Sunday, May 8, 2016 to Thursday, May 12, 2016 in Lima, Peru.

This is a great opportunity to:

  • Network with more than 800 representatives of the global securities market industry coming from around the world to Lima, Peru.
  • Interact and share experiences with experts in the securities market industry, and establish business relationships.
  • Engage in high-level discussions on the latest trends in the global securities and futures markets and the challenges they pose for regulators.
